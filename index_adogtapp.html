Adogtapp

Adopt the perfect dog for you

The main objetive of Adogtapp is to promote dog adoptions and help you to find and choose the dog more adecuated to your personality and life.

Find your favourites dogs and make an appointment to meet them

Adogtapp put at your disposition the most important information of the dog in adoption: behaviour and history. Furthermore you can make an appointment to see the chosen dogs.

Watch how is your dog adapting and share the info with the animal shelter

Write a diary of your dog to be sure it is adapting correctly and share the information with the animal shelter to keep in touch in case you need any help.
