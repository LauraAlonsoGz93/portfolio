Time Out

Barcelona’s song

Time Out is a magazine distributed in several citys of the world. It has a section called La Cançó de Barcelona (Barcelona’s song). This page include an opinion article that talks about a song of a local band or artist.

Mossos de esquadra by Tony El Gitano

This song talks about the constant presence and repression of the police in the neighborhoods of San Roque and La Mina in the 70’s - 80’s where a lot of gypsy families lived.

An illustration to reflect the song

The song concept is represented by a facade of a typical building of the neighborhood of La Mina. We can see the huge shadows of the police with truncheons in their hands, that transmit the feeling of oppression.
